Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-17 (draft)

What we collect

To generate your sticker pack, we process the photo you upload (of yourself, a couple, your family, or a pet) and the style/options you choose in the Telegram bot.

Your photo is deleted after use

Your photo is used only to generate your sticker pack. We do not keep a database of face templates ("faceprints"), and we do not use your photo to train AI models. Once your pack is generated, the photo is deleted from our systems.

To let you regenerate a pack without re-uploading, we may briefly keep a reference to your last photo (not a copy of the image itself) for a limited window after your pack is delivered. This reference expires automatically and is never used for anything beyond that regeneration request.

Payments

All payments are processed through Telegram Stars inside the bot. We do not receive or store your card details — Telegram handles the transaction. For refunds or payment issues, use /paysupport in the bot chat.

Regeneration guarantee

If your pack doesn't look like you, we regenerate it for free, subject to fair-use limits that prevent abuse of the guarantee.

AI-generated content

Your stickers are generated by AI image models. We do not apply invisible or steganographic watermarking today; a visible indication that a pack is AI-generated is part of our planned compliance work (EU AI Act Art. 50 disclosure requirements).

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing (for example under the EU GDPR or Russia's 152-ФЗ). Because your photo is deleted automatically after your pack is generated, there is typically nothing further to delete by the time you'd make such a request — but you can contact us with any privacy question or request.

Uploading photos of other people

Only upload photos you have the right to use: your own photo, photos of people close to you — with their consent, and photos of your own pet. Do not upload photos of people who haven't agreed to have stickers made of them.

Contact

Questions about this policy: reach us via the bot, or at the contact address published in the bot's Telegram profile.
