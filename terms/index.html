The service

StickerKit generates a personalized sticker pack from a photo you upload, using AI image generation, delivered through the Telegram bot as an installable Telegram sticker set.

Payment

Paid packs are purchased with Telegram Stars, inside the bot. Prices shown on this website are for reference; the price you actually pay is confirmed by the bot at checkout.

Refunds

For refund requests or payment disputes, use /paysupport in the bot chat. We handle Stars refunds in line with Telegram's payment platform rules.

Regeneration guarantee

If a delivered pack doesn't reasonably resemble the subject of your photo, we will regenerate it at no extra cost. This guarantee is for genuine likeness issues and is subject to fair-use limits to prevent abuse.

Your responsibilities

  • Only upload photos you have the right to use (yourself, or others with their consent).
  • Don't use the service to create sticker packs that are illegal, harassing, or impersonate someone without consent.
  • Don't attempt to abuse the free-preview or regeneration guarantee to obtain packs without payment.

AI-generated content

Stickers are generated by AI and may not perfectly reproduce the uploaded photo. We do not guarantee pixel-perfect likeness, only that we'll regenerate a pack that clearly doesn't resemble you, per the guarantee above.

Termination

We may suspend or stop providing the service to accounts that abuse it (fraud, abuse of guarantees, prohibited content).

Changes

We may update these terms; continued use of the bot after an update means you accept the revised terms.

Contact

Questions: reach us via the bot, or the contact address in the bot's Telegram profile.
