Frequently asked questions

Is my photo safe?

Your photo is used only to generate your pack and is deleted right after. We don't keep a face database or use your photo to train models.

Can I try it for free?

Yes: the first preview sticker is free — upload a photo, pick a style, and see the result before you pay.

What if the stickers don't look like me?

We'll regenerate your pack for free if it doesn't look like you — no extra charge.

How long does it take?

A couple of minutes from photo to finished pack, right inside the Telegram chat.

How do I pay and get a refund?

Payment is handled entirely by Telegram Stars inside the bot. For refunds or payment issues, use /paysupport in the chat.

What kind of photo works best?

A clear photo of the face in good light, without heavy filters, dark sunglasses, or things covering the face. For a couple or family, make sure every face is clearly visible.

What languages are supported?

English and Russian today, with Portuguese and Spanish on the way.
